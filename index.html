New Game Release: Kingdom Come Deliverance 2

Author: Dmytro Bailuk

Date: 2024-04-18

Category: New Releases

The continuation to the Czech historical RPG is here! How does it compare to modern RPGs and what is new?

Kingdom Come Deliverance 2 introduces a completely overhauled combat system, more intricate quest lines, and an expanded world nearly twice the size of the original game. Players can now engage in larger-scale battles and interact with more NPCs, each with unique backgrounds and stories.

With its rich historical accuracy, the game offers an immersive experience into the late medieval period, including dynamically changing weather, a day-night cycle that affects NPC routines, and historical events that influence the game's storyline.

Overwatch 2 April Balance Patch

Author: Alikhanov Miras

Date: 2024-04-17

Category: Updates

What did Blizzard cook for us this time?

This latest patch addresses several balance issues that have been prevalent in competitive play, introduces a new map set in the heart of Rome, and tweaks to character abilities that aim to bring underused heroes back into the meta.

The patch notes also include adjustments to the overall game mechanics, such as hit registration and latency improvements, which aim to provide a smoother and more responsive gaming experience.
